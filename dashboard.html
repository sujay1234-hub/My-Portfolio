Frequently Asked Questions
When does work start on my portfolio? ▼
Work begins immediately after payment is confirmed. UPI/bank details are sent within 30 minutes of order. Once you pay, the build starts right away.
How do I share my content? ▼
Upload files in the "Files" section (photo, resume PDF, screenshots) or send them on WhatsApp. The more you provide, the better we personalise your portfolio.
What's included in the free revision? ▼
One complete revision round — design tweaks, content changes, layout, colors, animations, and more. Use the "Request Revision" page to submit clearly.
How will I receive my portfolio? ▼
Full source code as a ZIP (downloadable from the Files section), a deployment guide PDF, and optionally a live hosted URL — all within 24 hours.
Can I upgrade my plan? ▼
Yes! WhatsApp or message us and we'll upgrade your plan. You only pay the difference. Upgrades are done within the hour.
What if I'm not satisfied? ▼
We include a free revision round. If you're still not happy we keep working until you are. Your satisfaction is our reputation.
